• A flick of the hand in mid-air moves pans left or right respectively.
  • A two hand flick up or down pans up or down respectively
  • The detection algorithm can determine the amount and position of motion. It will only pan if your amount of motion exceeds a threshold.
  • Try to keep the rest of your body still.
  • Your hand should preferably come within two to three feet away from the camera.
  • Gestures should be performed with open palms facing the camera.
  • The algorithm is roughly 80% accurate but mileage may vary!
